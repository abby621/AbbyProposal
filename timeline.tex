\subsection*{Project Timeline and Milestones}

This project is organized with an annual cycle of research, evaluation and deployment.  Annual milestones include the submission of research articles at the conclusion of the basic research and algorithm development, and the deployment of algorithmic improvements to the search prototype.  The remaining milestones are quarterly financial reporting and semi-annual progress reports.
